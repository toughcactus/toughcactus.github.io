二、文本补全 | company | company-box

  • 安装 | company

      (use-package company
    :ensure t
    :init (add-hook 'after-init-hook 'global-company-mode) ;; 全局有效
    :config
    (setq company-idle-delay                 0.08 ;; 提示时延
          company-minimum-prefix-length      2 ;; 触发提示的字符数
          company-show-numbers               t ;; 给选项编号 M-编号选择
  ;;        company-tooltip-limit           10 ;; 选项个数，超出10个无编号
          company-dabbrev-downcase nil ;; 关闭转小写
          ))

  • 安装 | company-box

      (use-package company-box
    :ensure t
    :if window-system
    :hook (company-mode . company-box-mode))
  • 默认配置中，补全时忽略大小写
  • 也可使用M-x customize-group进行插件配置

三、文本补全 | auto-complete

  • 安装

      (use-package auto-complete
    :ensure t
    :init
    (progn
      (ac-config-default)
      (global-auto-complete-mode t))
    :config (add-to-list 'ac-modes 'org-mode) ;; 添加org-mode有效
    )
